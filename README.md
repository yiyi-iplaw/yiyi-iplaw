### 关于我 · About

我是一名同时持有中国律师执业资格和美国加利福尼亚州律师执业资格的律师，专注于美国联邦知识产权诉讼、跨境电商争议、紧急禁令程序及 USPTO 商标事务。曾参与联邦巡回上诉法院、第十一巡回上诉法院及多个美国联邦地区法院的知识产权诉讼与上诉事务。

I am an attorney licensed in the People's Republic of China and the State of California, focusing on U.S. federal intellectual property litigation, cross-border e-commerce disputes, emergency injunctive proceedings, and USPTO trademark matters. My experience includes matters before the Federal Circuit, the Eleventh Circuit, and multiple U.S. district courts.
